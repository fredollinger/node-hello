Voyager 5200 Series Product Information

Article ID :
000023101


General Information
  • Status: Shipping
  • Date code location for this product
  • Warranty information
Features and Specifications
  • Talk time: Up to 7 hours
  • Standby time: Up to 9 days
  • Updateable firmware using Plantronics Hub
  • 4 mic active digital signal processing (DSP)
  • Internal, adaptive 20-band equalizer, acoustic echo cancellation, and sidetone detection
  • Smart Sensor technology
  • Multipoint technology
  • WindSmart technology
  • P2i nano-coating provides protection from moisture
  • HD voice clarity on wideband supported devices and networks
  • Headset battery meter for iPhone and iPad and enabled for Android using the Plantronics Hub app
  • Micro USB charging connection
  • Voice commands
  • Range: Up to 98 feet (30 meters)
  • Bluetooth v4.1
  • Headset weight: 20 grams/0.71 oz
  • Battery type: rechargeable non-replaceable lithium ion polymer
  • Charge time: 90 minutes
  • Bluetooth profiles: A2DP, AVRCP, HFP, HSP, PBAP, SSP2
  • Caller name announce (PBAP Bluetooth profile)
  • Optional charging case holds up to 14 hours of recharging
